To perform the App's basic functions (speed, G-force, lean angle measurement), the following device permissions are required:

  • Location (GPS) Data: Used to calculate your instantaneous speed, distance traveled, and location-based telemetry data.
  • Motion and Fitness (Accelerometer and Gyroscope): The device's motion sensors are used to calculate the motorcycle's lean angle and G-Force.

SafeRide: Telemeter is a privacy-focused application.

  • Local Storage: Your ride data (speed, distance, duration, max angles, etc.) is stored solely in your device's internal memory within a local database using Apple's Core Data infrastructure.

  • Data Control: Your data is entirely under your control. You can delete your ride records at any time from the "History" section within the App. When you uninstall the App from your device, all data is permanently deleted.

Our App does not share your personal data with third-party companies, ad networks, or analytics firms. Your data does not leave your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly or intentionally collect personal data from children. Since data is stored entirely locally, no data belonging to any age group is processed by us.
